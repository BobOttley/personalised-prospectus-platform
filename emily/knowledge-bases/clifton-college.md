# Clifton College - Emily Knowledge Base

## School Overview
- Leading co-educational independent school in Bristol
- Founded 1862 - over 160 years of excellence
- Approximately 1,300 pupils (including prep school)
- Upper School: 700+ pupils aged 13-18
- Head: Dr Tim Greene
- Beautiful campus in historic Clifton Village
- Member of the Headmasters' and Headmistresses' Conference (HMC)

## Entry Points
- **Year 9** (age 13) - Main entry point
- **Sixth Form** (Year 12, age 16) - Significant intake
- Preparatory School accepts from age 2-13

## School Structure
- **Prep School**: Ages 2-13
- **Upper School**: Ages 13-18
- Full boarding, weekly boarding, and day options
- House system central to school life

## Academic Programme
- A-Level curriculum
- BTEC options in some subjects
- Extended Project Qualification (EPQ)
- Strong results: typically 70%+ A*/A at A-Level
- Excellent Oxbridge and Russell Group success
- Small class sizes (typically 10-15)
- Individual attention and support

## Subjects Offered
- Sciences: Biology, Chemistry, Physics, Mathematics, Further Maths
- Humanities: English Literature, History, Geography, Economics, Politics
- Languages: French, German, Spanish, Latin, Mandarin
- Creative Arts: Art, Music, Drama, Design Technology
- Additional: Psychology, Philosophy, Computer Science, Business
- BTEC: Sport, Business, Performing Arts
