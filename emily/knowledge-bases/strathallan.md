# Strathallan School - Emily Knowledge Base

## School Overview
- Leading independent co-educational boarding and day school in Scotland
- Located in Forgandenny, Perthshire - 153 acres of beautiful Scottish countryside
- Founded 1913 by Harry Riley
- Around 600 pupils aged 5-18 (Prep and Senior)
- Head: Mark Lauder
- One of Scotland's top independent schools

## Entry Points
- **Strathallan Prep**: Reception to Year 6 (ages 5-11) - Riley House
- **Third Form**: Year 7 (age 11/12) - Entry to Senior School
- **Fourth Form**: Year 9 (age 13/14) - Main senior entry point, GCSE years begin
- **Sixth Form**: Year 12 (age 16) - A-Level entry

## Academic Programme - Scotland's Triple Pathway
- First school in Scotland to offer genuine curriculum choice
- **Scottish Highers**: Traditional Scottish route, ideal for Scottish universities. Study 5 Highers in S5, then Advanced Highers in S6
- **A-Levels**: English curriculum pathway, 3-4 subjects studied over 2 years
- **Combination**: Mix Scottish and English qualifications
- Wide subject choice at GCSE, Highers, and A-Level
- Small class sizes with individual attention
- Strong Oxbridge and Russell Group preparation

## GCSE/National 5 Subjects

## Boarding Life
- 7 boarding houses across the campus
- **Boys' Houses**: Freeland, Nicol, Ruthven, Simpson
- **Girls' Houses**: Glenbrae, Thornbank, Woodlands
- **Prep House**: Riley House (co-ed for younger pupils)
- Full boarding, weekly boarding, and flexi-boarding options
- House parents and tutors provide excellent pastoral care
- Weekend activities and trips
- Modern facilities and comfortable accommodation
- Strong house spirit and inter-house competitions

## Flexi-Boarding
- Best of both worlds - boarding some nights, home others
- Regular pattern (e.g., Tuesday and Thursday nights)
- Ad hoc nights available
- Great for trying boarding or managing busy family schedules
- Full access to evening activities and house life
