## Admissions

### Entry Points
- Main entry: Nursery and Reception
- Spaces may be available in other year groups
- Birthday cut-off: 31st August

### Application Process
1. Attend Open Morning or contact Admissions (0207 591 4628)
2. Register online at applications.cognita.com
3. Assessment day - Nursery: stay and play sessions; Prep: assessments in English, maths, reasoning
4. Offers typically made end of January

### Registration
- Register at least one year in advance for Nursery
- Nursery entry does not guarantee Prep entry
