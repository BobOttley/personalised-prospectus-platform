# Cheltenham Ladies' College - Emily Knowledge Base

## School Overview
- Leading independent girls' school in Cheltenham, Gloucestershire
- Founded 1853 - over 170 years of excellence
- Around 900 girls aged 11-18
- Students from over 40 countries
- Executive Principal: Eve Jardine-Young MA
- One of the top girls' schools in the UK

## Entry Points
- **Lower College**: Year 7 (age 11) - Main entry point for younger students
- **Upper College**: Year 9 (age 13) - Significant intake at this stage
- **Sixth Form**: Year 12 (age 16) - Popular entry point for A-Levels or IB

## Academic Programme
- Offers both **A-Levels** and **IB Diploma Programme**
- 94% of students achieve first-choice university place
- Strong results across all subjects
- Average class size: 12-15 students
- Wide subject choice at GCSE, A-Level and IB
- Exceptional STEM, humanities, and arts departments
- Oxbridge success: Regular students to Oxford and Cambridge
- Russell Group universities: 85%+ go to Russell Group

## A-Levels Offered
Sciences: Biology, Chemistry, Physics, Mathematics, Further Maths
Humanities: English Literature, History, Geography, Politics, Economics
Languages: French, German, Spanish, Latin, Greek, Mandarin
Arts: Art, Music, Drama & Theatre Studies, History of Art
Others: Psychology, Philosophy, Religious Studies, Computer Science

## IB Diploma
- Full International Baccalaureate programme
- Average IB points: typically 38-40 (out of 45)
- Extended Essay, Theory of Knowledge, CAS (Creativity, Activity, Service)
- Ideal for well-rounded students who want breadth

## Application Process
1. Register interest via website
2. Attend Open Day or private visit
3. Submit application form
4. Entrance assessments (age-appropriate)
5. Interview
6. Offer made
7. Accept place and pay deposit

## Key Dates
- Open Days typically in autumn and spring
- Registration deadline usually December for following September entry
- Assessments in January
- Offers typically March
