# Bloxham School - Emily Knowledge Base

## School Overview
- Independent co-educational boarding and day school in Oxfordshire
- Located in Bloxham village, near Banbury - 60 acres of beautiful countryside
- Founded 1860 as part of the Woodard Corporation
- Under 600 pupils aged 11-18
- Headmaster: Paul Sanderson (since 2013)
- Motto: "Justorum Semita Lux Splendens" (The path of the just is a shining light)

## Five Hallmarks of a Bloxham Education
1. **A Passion for Learning** - Fostering creativity and capacity for dynamic, independent and critical thought
2. **A Balanced Curriculum** - Challenging academics complemented by broader curriculum inspiring curiosity, rigour and ambition
3. **Christian Values** - Nurturing compassionate spirit so students become a force for good
4. **Gold Standard Pastoral Care** - Nurturing wellbeing within our boarding community
5. **Development of Character** - Promoting responsibility, generosity, resilience and imagination

## Sport

- 2 full-size AstroTurf pitches
- 7 rugby pitches
- Swimming pool

### Main Sports by Term

- One of the finest programmes in the region
- National Schools Plate Winners 2018/19

### Hockey
- Largest and most popular sport at Bloxham
