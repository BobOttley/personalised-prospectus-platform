From the Headmaster

A Personal Welcome

Dear Mr and Mrs Ashworth,

Thank you for your interest in Bloxham School. It gives me great pleasure to share this personalised prospectus, created especially for Henry and your family.

At Bloxham, we believe that the individual matters. With fewer than 600 students, we are small enough to ensure that Henry receives the personal attention he deserves, yet large enough to offer every opportunity a school twice our size could provide.

Founded in 1860, Bloxham has been nurturing young people for over 160 years. Our five hallmarks of education - a passion for learning, a balanced curriculum, Christian values, gold standard pastoral care, and character development - provide the foundation for Henry to flourish both academically and personally.

I would be delighted to welcome you and Henry to experience Bloxham for yourselves. Whether through a personal visit or one of our Open Morning events, I am confident you will feel the warmth and sense of community that makes our school so special.

Paul Sanderson

Headmaster

Sport

Henry and Sport at Bloxham

Where passion meets excellence on the playing fields

🏈

Henry and Rugby

Bloxham's rugby programme is one of the finest in the region. With 15 teams fielded across all age groups, Henry will find his place regardless of ability. Excellent facilities, dedicated strength and conditioning coaches, and a 7-a-side programme in the Lent term ensure comprehensive development for every player.

Recent Achievements

  • National Schools Plate Winners 2018/19
  • National Finals 2019/20
  • 15 teams fielded in 2021
🏒

Henry and Hockey

Hockey is the largest and most popular sport at Bloxham, offering challenging fixtures against well-reputed schools. Boys play in the Lent term on our two full-size AstroTurf pitches. Strong links with Banbury Hockey Club provide additional opportunities, with several hockey specialists on staff to develop Henry's skills.

🏏

Henry and Cricket

Cricket is highly popular at Bloxham with five senior teams competing in the summer term. Professional coaching from George Setterfield ensures Henry can develop his skills year-round, with indoor nets available in Michaelmas and pre-season coaching in Lent term. Our excellent grass pitches and indoor facilities provide the perfect environment for players at all levels.

Dewey Sports Centre

4-court sports hall with spring-loaded flooring, fitness suite, and climbing wall with 9 routes.

Playing Fields

2 full-size AstroTurf pitches, 7 rugby pitches, and extensive grass playing fields.

Swimming Pool

Indoor swimming pool supporting competitive swimming, triathlon training, and recreational use.
