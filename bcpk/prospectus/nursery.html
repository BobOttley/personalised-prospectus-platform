Dear Parent/Guardian,

Thank you for your interest in our Nursery for your child. These early years are precious - a time of wonder, discovery, and first friendships.

A Day in the Life

Arts & Crafts

Painting, drawing, collaging, and creating - your child would explore their creativity through a rich variety of art activities every day.

Music & Movement

Singing, dancing, and making music together. Your child would develop rhythm, coordination, and the joy of musical expression.

Roleplay & Imagination

From home corners to construction zones, your child would let their imagination soar through creative play.

Early Reading & Writing

Stories, songs, and mark-making. Your child would begin their literacy journey in the most natural, playful way.

Outdoor Learning

Every day, your child would explore our dedicated outdoor spaces - developing social skills and a love of nature through play.

Dance & Sport

Movement, games, and physical development. Your child would build coordination, strength, and confidence through active play.

Even our youngest children enjoy Forest School. Your child would explore nature, build confidence, and develop a lifelong love of the outdoors.

Nature & Wildlife

Birds, bugs, flowers, and trees - your child would discover the natural world right in the heart of London.

Running, climbing, hiding, seeking - our Secret Garden gives your child the space they need to grow and explore.
