Culture of Kindness

Kindness is "the currency at Brighton College." From early empathy lessons to community volunteering, Oliver would learn that being kind is as important as being clever.

Two acres of outdoor learning in the heart of London - where Oliver would explore, discover, and grow.

Our dedicated Forest School programme brings learning outdoors. Oliver would build dens, identify wildlife, light fires safely, and develop resilience through adventure - all within our beautiful garden.

Outdoor Classrooms

Learning doesn't stop at the garden gate. Maths in the meadow, science among the trees, stories by the pond - Oliver's classroom would extend into nature every day.
