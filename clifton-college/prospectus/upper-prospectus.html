Founded 1862

A Heritage of Excellence

Clifton College was founded by Dr John Percival, a Victorian visionary who championed science over classics, the education of girls, and equality for all.

We were the first traditional boys' public school to become fully co-educational in 1987, and remain one of only 26 schools to hold the distinction of being an original English public school.
