At Cheltenham Ladies' College, our mission is to support and guide our pupils in becoming self-determining, fulfilled and resilient women who value, serve and enrich their communities.

We are an independent school for girls aged 11 to 18, based in Cheltenham, Gloucestershire. We offer both boarding and day places for pupils.

Our fees include many costs associated with providing a thorough education at College, including teaching, essential educational materials and text books, and many curriculum trips. Fees also include the provision of pastoral care, Personal Accident Insurance, meals and snacks, and many other activities as part of the co-curricular and weekend programmes.

Fees for attending Cheltenham Ladies' College

Fees are termly, and charged in advance at the start of each term. The fees shown below apply to the 2025-26 academic year are inclusive of VAT.

Lower College and Upper College,  and Sixth Form pupils who transition from Upper College in September 2025:

  • £20,100 Boarding per term

  • £12,660 Day per term

Sixth Form pupils new to CLC:

  • £22,290 Boarding per term

  • £14,340 Day per term

Parents who joined CLC's Fees in Advance Scheme prior to 29 July 2024 will be notified of the applicable College Fees separately.

Support with Fees

CLC offers a bursary scheme, The Beale Awards, which offers up to 100% fee support. All financial awards are means tested and include pro-rated assistance towards the additional costs of attending, such as uniform and co-curricular activities.
