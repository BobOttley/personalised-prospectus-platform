Welcome

Cheltenham Ladies' College is a leading independent girls day and boarding school, for pupils aged 11-18 years old.

Founded in Gloucestershire over 170 years ago, CLC is renowned for its pioneering approach to girls education, and is consistently rated one of the top girls schools in the UK.

A CLC education is full of joy, excitement and challenge. It encourages pupils to think globally, to cherish diversity and to innovate in the face of an ever-changing world.
