Term Dates

Here at Cheltenham Ladies' College, all girls are expected to be present from the beginning to the end of term. Leave of absence is granted only in exceptional circumstances and must be sought in advance from Miss Caroline Ralph, Head of Pastoral Care.

We are an independent boarding and day school for girls, based in Cheltenham, Gloucestershire. We have 3 terms; Autumn, Spring and Summer.

Dates are subject to change prior to the start of the Autumn Term.

Parents: For a printable version of this document, including details of Parents’ Meetings, or to access the school calendar, please visit My School Portal.
