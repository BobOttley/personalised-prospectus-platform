New Sixth Form Centre

Our new Sixth Form Centre development is due to open in Autumn 2026!

How will it support students?

Through physical space and design, the new centre will promote independent and self-directed learning, creating a strong foundation for adulthood and preparation for life at university. It will enable students to develop academic confidence, self-motivation and resilience, through real-world professional and academic experiences, and authentic environments that mirror a university education.

Beyond this, the new centre will support our holistic approach to fostering emotional, creative, and spiritual growth beyond academic learning.

We have to continually evolve to ensure that we are best equipping pupils for the future. We are preparing them for the workforce of the 2030s, the 2040s, and beyond.
Dr David Gamblin
Vice Principal Academic

What will be included?

Collaborative Learning Spaces

Digital learning labs, creative studios, and collaborative spaces, will ensure students can work collaboratively and engage with advanced academic and interdisciplinary materials, supporting scholarly curiosity.

Employability Suite

Spaces that simulate real-world professional and academic contexts will challenge students to apply knowledge practically, preparing for life beyond CLC.

Pods for Focused Learning

Learning Pods will encourage self-directed study and independent learning, supporting a smooth psychological and academic transition to higher education.

Wellbeing and Relaxation Zones

Shared social spaces, comfortable common rooms, and welcoming caf√©-style areas will foster community, encourage informal discussion, and help build deep friendships.

Break-out and Seminar Rooms

Collaborative project zones, seminar spaces and break-out rooms will encourage teamwork and creativity, preparing students for higher education, global citizenship and adult working life.

Creative Spaces

Inviting spaces, including outdoor areas, will provide opportunities for creativity, contemplation and reflection, encouraging emotional maturity.

Community Areas

Integrated community service spaces and creative outreach hubs will support our partnerships with the community and businesses, helping students connect their learning to meaningful, real-world impact.
