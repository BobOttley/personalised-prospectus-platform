Professional Guidance

Our Professional Guidance Centre (PGC) runs comprehensive programmes to support girls applying for higher education, both in the UK and abroad, including specialist provision for US and Oxbridge applications. Higher education and careers events include careers and admissions talks, mock interviews, conferences, networking dinners, and CV writing workshops.

Supported by the PGC, over the last 10 years more than 1,400 students have graduated from CLC Sixth Form and have gone on to study at over 170 universities across the world.

Read more about the PGC

UK Universities

In 2023, 77% of our leavers went on to study at 35 different universities across the UK.

Of those who went on to study at UK institutions, two-thirds have taken up places at the following top universities:

  • The University of Cambridge

  • UCL (University College London)

  • Imperial College London

  • The University of Edinburgh

  • Durham University

  • KCL (King's College London)

  • University of Warwick

  • University of Bristol

  • University of Glasgow

Each year, a number of students are also accepted into top UK colleges specialising in music, drama, art and design, fashion, film, theatre, agriculture or veterinary science.

Global Destinations

A further 10% of our Class of 2023 opted to study in North America. Their most popular university destinations were the University of California, New York University and the University of Southern California, with places also secured at 8 other Colleges including Brown, Columbia University, Dartmouth, Pratt Institute and UPenn.

Each year, a small number of pupils also secure places to study in Europe and Asia. The 2023 Class are going on to study in at universities and colleges in Hong Kong, Spain, Sweden and The Netherlands.

Guild Members Worldwide

On leaving College, our alumnae become Guild members. Established in 1884, Guild is our association of former pupils and it currently has over 8,500 active members in more than 70 countries worldwide.

Guild membership is free and offers many benefits including events, careers support and networking opportunities. If our alumnae need careers advice or are moving to a new location around the world, then other Guild members are often happy to help. You can find out more about our alumnae network on the CLC Guild website.
