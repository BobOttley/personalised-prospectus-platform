The academic departments at College take pride in both their excellent results and the range of enriching lessons and activities they offer. Pupils are given a broad education as they progress from LC1 (Year 7) to SFC2 (Year 13) and our departments guide them through exams in more than 30 subjects. Departments also organise conferences, co-curricular clubs and trips.

The students study for GCSEs / IGCSEs in UC4 and UC5 (Years 10-11), and in SFC1 and SFC2 (Sixth Form) they opt for either A Levels or the International Baccalaureate (IB) Diploma Programme. Unlike many schools, we do not have predetermined options blocks. Instead, with guidance, students choose their subject combination and the timetable is then constructed.
