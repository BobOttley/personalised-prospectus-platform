Visiting

TRAVELLING BY CAR

Please use GL50 3AA if using a Satnav. This will take you to our Parabola Arts Centre, which is directly opposite the Main Entrance to College. For mid-week appointments, parking is by prior arrangement. On Open Days, please use our Health and Fitness Centre car park. Pay and display parking can also be found on most nearby roads or in a large car park in St George's Road, two minutes' walk from College.

TRAVELLING BY RAIL

Cheltenham Spa Station is approximately 20 minutes' walk or five minutes' drive from College. Taxis are usually available at the station without the need to pre-book. There are regular direct trains from many major stations across the UK, including London Paddington, Bristol Temple Meads, Birmingham New Street, Swindon, Reading, Cardiff Central, Manchester Piccadilly, Leeds, and Edinburgh.

SPORTS FACILITIES

Our sports facilities, CLC Health and Fitness Centre and CLC Well Place, are located approximately 10 minutes' walk from the main College site. Parking is available at the Health and Fitness Centre (GL50 2NX) for both facilities.
