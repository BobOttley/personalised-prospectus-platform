Upper College

The GCSE years

Upper College is where academic focus intensifies while maintaining the breadth of experience that makes CLC special.

Years 12-13

Sixth Form College

Where Sophia could flourish

Sixth Form at CLC offers a unique choice: A-Levels or the International Baccalaureate. Both pathways lead to outstanding university outcomes.

A-Levels

Sophia could choose 3-4 subjects to study in depth. Our 2024 A-Level cohort achieved 70% A*-A grades, with exceptional results across sciences, humanities, and creative subjects.

International Baccalaureate

Or the IB Diploma could give Sophia a broader education across six subjects, plus Theory of Knowledge, Extended Essay, and Creativity, Activity, Service (CAS). Our 2024 average of 39.1 points places us 3rd in the UK.

New Sixth Form Centre

Opening Autumn 2026 - Sophia would have access to a stunning new facility designed specifically for Sixth Formers, with study spaces, social areas, and state-of-the-art resources.
