With a wider age bracket and seasonal themes, it's a storytelling adventure the whole family can enjoy together for the first time.At Strathallan Prep School, we believe in the power of storytelling to ignite young imaginations. Story Explorers takes young readers on a storytelling adventure designed to capture the imagination and spark a lifelong love of literature.A Very Special Christmas SessionIn our first session for the 2025-26 academic year, we are inviting explorers aged 0-6 to join us in a uniquely festive event, adventuring through The Nicht Afore Christmas. This is a beautiful Scots language translation of the classic Christmas poem, and we are delighted to be joined by the original translator, Irene McFarlane, for a live reading session bringing this classic tale life – in Scots – for the youngest of readers. This event, brought to life with the kind support of Tippermuir Books, is a celebration not only of Christmas magic, but of local language, culture and community heritage.Your Story Explorer will enjoy: - Elf-led crafts and storytelling activities - Hot chocolate and festive treats - A live reading from Irene - A special gift from Santa for every child This is a joyful, immersive morning for families to share together and a beautiful introduction to the richness of Scots storytelling traditions.Next Session:The Nicht Afore Christmas Saturday 13th December 09.00–11.00 £5 per child (grown-ups go free) Register nowRefreshments for all ages will be provided.
